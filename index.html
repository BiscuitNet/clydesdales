About

Join the herd of Clydesdales, a special donkey and the beautiful Peggy on a trip to remember...

Experience life on the croft, in an idyllic location 7 miles north of Ullapool on the West Coast of Scotland, making us a great stop on your North Coast 500 trip!


Adults can meet our 6 Clydesdales giants: Donald, Angus, Finlay, Hamish, Bruce and Archie!
Groom and get to know your horse while out on a scenic walk. There will be masses of photo opportunities for you with your Clydesdale. 
Peggy and Dougal can also come along for the craic!

In the Kids Corner -
Peggy the fallow fawn is ready for cuddles and being hand fed! Dougal the Donkey for feeding and grooming.
Children are also welcome to feed the chickens and collect and keep any eggs that are discovered!

Visit

On arrival you will be introduced to your Clydesdale and after a talk on safety, and how to lead your Clydesdale, we will set off on a 30-40-minute walk, taking in the picturesque highland scenery.

You will walk with your Clydesdale (not ride) on a flat, single track quiet road and there will be lots of time to stop and take photos of you with your Clydesdale in the beautiful surroundings.

After the walk, we will take the Clydesdales home to the croft, where you will be taught how to groom and look after these gentle giants and experience them up-close and personal.
Overall the experience will be approximately 90 minutes.

Refreshments are provided, please email with any dietary requirements before your visit and we will be delighted to assist.
